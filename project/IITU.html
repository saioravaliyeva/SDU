Khalygaralyk akparatttyk tekhnolar university—ñ) - was established in April 2009 on behalf of the President of the Republic of Kazakhstan, in cooperation with the educational organization iCarnegie, representing the American IT University Carnegie Mellon University.

International cooperation of the university is carried out through the improvement of the system of training specialists in accordance with international standards, advanced training of the teaching staff, the use of new technologies and advanced experience in educational and research activities through cooperation with foreign universities under direct contracts
